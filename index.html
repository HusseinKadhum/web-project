    The world of Bioshock throws the player into a lost utopia beneath the sea. The vast City of Rapture housed thousands of the Earths brightest minds, skilled artists and hardest workers. The cream of the crop! The reins of Governments and policies arent long enough to reach down here. For a time it was a society of prosperity and radical thinking. But these tales always have a twist.
    The colony under the sea just needed something to divide them. Societal problems of the surface made their way down with the people. After a short period of time the occupants were bickering with one another and with word of an outsider planning an overthrow, Rapture was ready to pop! Then came the discovery that could have changed mankind, a type of slug with a miraculous healing ability. A doctor whom had been brought down to try analyse & calm the situation got backing from the citys biggest businessmen (whom was also crooked as a bent nail) to further research and understand the slugs abilities. The understanding was that all profits gained would belong to the backer.
    The discoveries were astounding to both parties. The slug secreted a fluid that opened the path to D.N.A. manipulation. Diseases were curable and man could control the elements! From this point Rapture took a dark turn. The new miracle juice christened 'Adam' could be harvested with a much higher yield if the slug was implanted in a human host. For some reason young girls were most suited for implantation. This was the point of no return. Parents were deceived into giving their children in for this grizzly cause.
    Large amounts of Adam were now in production, greed and fear took hold. The key figures began to see one another as threats and so did their followers, the city fell into civil war and bodies lay around every second corner. The young host girls were sent out to gather the remaining Adam left in the corpses, although some citizens were still trying to live a normal life (under the sea) most had developed a heavy dependency for the wonder the drug. Many turned to desperate measures in order to get the Adam from the young girls. Robust bodyguards were created to protect the young girls while they eerily went from body to body. Present day Rapture is a chaos zone with no order and many addicts looking for their next hit and violence is their type of communication. Enter a stranger, an outsider.
